Head of state: King Letsie III

King Letsie III succeeded his father, King Moshoeshoe II, who was dethroned in 1990.Five years later, after the return to civilian government and amid political instability, he abdicated and his father was reinstated as monarch.Letsie III was restored as king in 1996 after his father died in a car accident. The monarch has no legislative or executive powers.
